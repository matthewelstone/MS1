About

Me and The Moon are about to release Strangers, the first single taken from their upcoming EP insert name. Early supporters of the band include BBC Introducing, Charlie Ashcroft (Amazing Radio) and John Kennedy with regular playlisting on Radio X. Named as one of Bandcamp’s ‘New and Notable’ with previous dates including multiple slots supporting indie favourites Flyte, Lewis Watson, Marika Hackman and Marsicans.

Strangers comes accompanied by a striking and timeless video shot on the Isle Of Wight and directed by James Sharp, harnessing the concept of isolation and change portrayed in the direct and painfully honest lyricism.

The alt-pop duo consist of Tamara Grzegorzek and Jonny Elstone who are currently based on the South Coast of the UK. The pair craft the full band arrangements together in their home studio, ready for high energy live shows with their 5 piece band or a stripped back duo setup consisting of jangling guitars and haunting harmonies.
